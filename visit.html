Visit Our Museum

Entrance is free for all!

We Offer Guided Tours

  • There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour.
  • 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.
  • If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.
  • Payment is done at the museum.

Food and Drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more. Our café offers a comfortable and welcoming space where you can relax and recharge after exploring our exhibits.

We source our ingredients locally and prioritize sustainability, so you can feel good about the food you're eating.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.

Stop by our museum shop to pick up a souvenir from your visit or to browse our selection of science-themed gifts and educational materials.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. We are committed to making our museum accessible to all visitors, regardless of ability.

If you have any specific accessibility needs, please don't hesitate to contact us before your visit. We will do everything we can to ensure that you have a comfortable and enjoyable experience at our museum.
